// THE GAP

Opportunity shouldn't stop at your school gate.

Most students only ever see what their own school surfaces. F.I.R.E breaks that ceiling — it's a discovery engine and an engagement hub in one: search, filter and register for opportunities across schools, clubs, competitions and community initiatives that actually match your interests and goals.

Co-founded and built end-to-end as a full-stack web application, then awarded the IB Global Youth Action Fund to fuel its expansion across Singapore.
